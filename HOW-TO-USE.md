# DonutNV McDonough Website — How To Use It

Your website is live at: **https://donutnv-mcdonough.vercel.app**

Customers can see your schedule, today's location, announcements, and request
bookings. You update everything yourself — no computer skills needed.

---

## How to log in (do this on your phone or computer)

1. Open a web browser and go to:
   **https://donutnv-mcdonough.vercel.app/admin**
2. Type the password: **(ask the owner)**
3. Tap **Log In**. You stay logged in for 30 days, so you won't have to type it
   often.

You'll see three tabs: **Schedule**, **Announcements**, **Bookings**.

---

## 1. Add where the truck will be (Schedule tab)

1. On the left, fill in the form:
   - **Event name** — e.g. "McDonough Square Market"
   - **Location name** — e.g. "McDonough Square"
   - **Address** — the full street address (this makes a map link for customers)
   - **Date**, **Start** time, **End** time
   - **Public or private?**
     - *Public* = anyone can come (shows in blue)
     - *Private* = a booked party (shows in red, but details still listed)
   - **Notes** (optional) — e.g. "Find us by the gazebo!"
2. Tap **Add stop**. It instantly appears on the website for everyone.

To **change or remove** a stop: find it in the list on the right and tap
**Edit** or **Delete**.

---

## 2. Post a last-minute update (Announcements tab)

Use this for cancellations or changes — it shows as a colored banner at the very
top of the website.

1. Type your message, e.g. "Today's 4pm stop is cancelled due to rain — sorry!"
2. Choose **Alert (red)** for important stuff or **Info (blue)** for general news.
3. Tap **Post**.

When it no longer applies, tap **Hide** (to keep it for later) or **Delete**.

---

## 3. See who wants to book you (Bookings tab)

When someone fills out the "Book Us" form on the website, it shows up here with
their name, email, phone, event date, and message. Just tap their email or phone
to reply.

---

## If you forget the password

Call/text your son — he can look it up or change it in seconds. You never lose
access.

---

That's it! Update your schedule whenever it changes, and customers will always
have the right info without you posting on Instagram every time.
